Anthropic API Key

To use AI calorie lookup (when you don't know exact calories), connect your Anthropic API key. It's stored only on your device — never sent anywhere except to Anthropic when you tap "Estimate."

How to get a key
1

Create an Anthropic account

Visit console.anthropic.com and sign up. It's free to create an account.

2

Add credit

Go to Plans & Billing in the console. Calorie lookups cost roughly $0.001–0.003 each — $5 of credit lasts a long time.

3

Generate an API key

Go to API Keys in the console and click Create Key. Copy the key (starts with sk-ant-).

4

Paste it above

Paste the key in the field above and tap Save. It's stored locally on your phone only.

5

Use AI estimate

When logging a meal or workout, type a description and tap Estimate with AI. Claude will look up calories for you.

Data

Everything is stored locally on your iPhone. Use these buttons to back up or restore your data.

Install on iPhone

1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome).
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).
3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4. Tap Add. The app icon will appear on your home screen and open full-screen like a native app.
